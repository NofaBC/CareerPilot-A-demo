  1. Capture your target role, location, and salary range.
  2. Extract skills from your resume or LinkedIn-style bio.
  3. Generate sample job matches ranked by fit score.
  4. Preview a mock “AutoApply Queue™” for selected roles.
  5. Run a mock interview prep session with likely questions.
